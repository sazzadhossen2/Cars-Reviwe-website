Car Details


The BMW 5 Series is a well-known luxury sedan that's part of BMW's midsize executive car lineup. As of my last knowledge update in September 2021, here are some general specifications for the BMW 5 Series. Keep in mind that the specifications may vary depending on the model year and trim level, so it's a good idea to check the latest information from BMW's official website or a dealership for the most up-to-date details:

Model: BMW 5 Series

Body Style: Sedan (the 5 Series also includes wagon and GT models, but the sedan is most common)

Engine Options: - Various engine options, including turbocharged four-cylinder and six-cylinder engines, as well as hybrid variants. - High-performance M5 models with high-performance engines.

Transmission: Typically an 8-speed automatic, with manual shifting mode.

Drive Type: Rear-wheel drive (RWD) or all-wheel drive (xDrive).

Interior: - Seating capacity: Typically 5 passengers. - High-quality materials, leather upholstery options. - Advanced infotainment system with a large touchscreen display. - Dual-zone or multi-zone climate control. - Available advanced driver-assistance features, including features like adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and more.

Exterior: - Features BMW 's classic kidney grille. - Various wheel options and paint colors. - Optional features like LED headlights, sunroof, and aerodynamic elements.

Performance: - Acceleration: 0 to 60 mph times vary by engine and trim. - Sporty handling and responsive steering. - Adaptive suspension options for improved ride quality and performance.

Safety: - Standard safety features typically include adaptive cruise control, forward collision warning, automatic emergency braking, lane departure warning, and more. - Additional advanced safety features may be available in higher trim levels or as part of optional packages.

Fuel Efficiency: - Miles per gallon (MPG) can vary significantly depending on the engine choice and driving conditions.

Price Range: - The price of a BMW 5 Series can vary greatly based on the specific model, trim level, and optional features. Typically, prices can range from mid-range luxury to higher-end luxury. Please note that the specifications for a BMW 5 Series can change from year to year, and BMW may introduce new features or updates in newer models. For the most current and detailed information on a specific BMW 5 Series model, visit the official BMW website or contact a BMW dealership.
