Car Details


The Mercedes-Benz G-Class, often referred to simply as the "G-Wagen" or "G-Class," is an iconic luxury SUV known for its distinctive design, off-road capabilities, and premium features. As of my last knowledge update in September 2021, here are some general specifications for the Mercedes-Benz G-Class

Model: Mercedes-Benz G-Class

Body Style: SUV

Engine Options: - Various engine options, including turbocharged V8 and V12 engines. - High-performance AMG variants with even more powerful engines.

Transmission: Typically a 9-speed automatic transmission.

Drive Type: All-wheel drive (AWD)

Interior: - Seating capacity: Typically 5 passengers, with some models offering seating for up to 7 passengers. - High-quality materials and premium leather upholstery. - Advanced infotainment system with a large touchscreen display. - Luxury features such as heated and ventilated seats, premium audio systems, and advanced driver-assistance features.

Exterior: - Iconic boxy and rugged design. - Various wheel options, grille designs, and exterior color choices. - Optional features like LED headlights, sunroof, and running boards.

Performance: - Acceleration: 0 to 60 mph times vary by engine and model. - Strong off-road capabilities, including a robust four-wheel-drive system. - Exceptional handling for an SUV of its size.

Safety: - Standard safety features typically include adaptive cruise control, forward collision warning, automatic emergency braking, and lane departure warning. - Additional advanced safety features may be available in higher trim levels or as part of optional packages.

Fuel Efficiency: - Fuel efficiency can vary significantly depending on the engine choice and driving conditions.

Price Range: - The price of a Mercedes-Benz G-Class can vary widely based on the specific model, trim level, and optional features. G-Class vehicles are positioned at the high end of the luxury SUV market and tend to be relatively expensive. Please note that the specifications for the Mercedes-Benz G-Class can change from year to year, and Mercedes-Benz may introduce new features or updates in newer models. For the most current and detailed information on a specific Mercedes-Benz G-Class model, visit the official Mercedes-Benz website or contact a Mercedes-Benz dealership.
